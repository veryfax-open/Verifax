## 🌐 English

### What is VERIFAX?

VERIFAX is an open source *hybrid, predictive and actionable* anti-corruption system designed to eliminate human bias in public procurement. It automates contractor evaluation, predicts risks before they occur, protects document integrity through blockchain, and generates concrete actions at every stage of the contracting process.

### The Three Pillars

| Pillar | What It Means | Example |
|---|---|---|
| 🔀 *Hybrid* | Public contracts, private contracts with public funds, and PPP | One system for concessions, PPP, and direct procurement |
| 🔮 *Predictive* | Projects future risk using machine learning | "This contractor has 78% probability of non-compliance" |
| ⚡ *Actionable* | Every alert comes with concrete recommended steps | "Request additional guarantees and notify the Comptroller now" |
### The Problem

In Latin America, public corruption in procurement causes billions in losses every year. Politicians influence contract awards, officials receive bribes, and society receives only a fraction of what was originally budgeted. The problem is not a lack of laws or data — it is the lack of systems that cross-check that data automatically, predict risk, and tell decision-makers exactly what to do next.

### What VERIFAX Does

- 📊 *Automated contractor scoring* — crosses public databases to generate an objective 0–100 risk score in seconds
- 🔮 *Predictive risk modeling* — machine learning trained on historical procurement data to forecast contractor behavior
- 📄 *Document integrity* — every tender document is hashed on blockchain at publication; any modification is immediately detectable
- 🔐 *Role-based access control* — citizens, contractors, officials, and oversight bodies each see exactly what they should
- 🤖 *AI anomaly detection* — flags collusion patterns, shell companies, and conflicts of interest automatically
- ⚡ *Actionable alerts* — every risk flag comes with specific recommended actions
- 👁️ *Citizen transparency portal* — any citizen can follow a procurement process in real time
- 🚨 *Automatic notifications* — notifies oversight bodies instantly when risk thresholds are exceeded

## 🇨🇴 Español

### ¿Qué es VERIFAX?

VERIFAX es un sistema tecnológico de código abierto, *híbrido, predictivo y accionable*, diseñado para eliminar el sesgo humano en la contratación pública. Automatiza la evaluación de contratistas, predice riesgos antes de que ocurran, protege la integridad documental mediante blockchain y genera acciones concretas en cada etapa del proceso contractual.

### Los Tres Pilares

| Pilar | Qué significa | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|
| 🔀 *Híbrido* | Contratos públicos, privados con recursos públicos y APP | Un sistema para concesiones, APP y contratación directa |
| 🔮 *Predictivo* | Proyecta el riesgo futuro con machine learning | "Este contratista tiene 78% de probabilidad de incumplir" |
| ⚡ *Accionable* | Cada alerta trae pasos concretos recomendados | "Solicita garantías adicionales y notifica a la Contraloría ahora" |

### Qué hace VERIFAX

- 📊 *Score automático* — cruza DIAN, RUP, SARLAFT, Procuraduría para generar puntaje de 0 a 100 en segundos
- 🔮 *Modelado predictivo* — machine learning entrenado con históricos del SECOP
- 📄 *Integridad documental* — cada pliego sellado en blockchain; cualquier modificación es detectable
- 🔐 *Control de acceso por roles* — cada actor ve exactamente lo que debe ver
- 🤖 *Detección de anomalías* — identifica colusión, empresas fachada y conflictos de interés
- ⚡ *Alertas accionables* — cada alerta incluye acciones específicas recomendadas
- 👁️ *Portal ciudadano* — seguimiento en tiempo real para cualquier ciudadano
- 🚨 *Notificaciones automáticas* — alerta a entes de control automáticamente

### Contexto Inicial
Piloto en *Colombia* con integración al ecosistema SECOP, diseñado para escalar a *América Latina* bajo estándares OCDS.
